Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on our website, make a booking, or contact us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and appointment preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide and maintain our services, manage your bookings, communicate with you, and improve our website experience.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Terms & Conditions

1. Booking Policy

Appointments must be booked at least 24 hours in advance. Cancellations should be made at least 12 hours before the scheduled time to avoid cancellation fees.

2. Payment Terms

Payment is due at the time of service. We accept cash, credit cards, and mobile payments. Prices are subject to change without prior notice.

3. Late Arrivals

Clients arriving more than 15 minutes late may need to reschedule their appointment to ensure quality service for all clients.

4. Liability

Thusha Saloon is not liable for any allergic reactions to products. Please inform your stylist of any allergies or sensitivities before treatment.
